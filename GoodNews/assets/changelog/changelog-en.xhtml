4.0.0

Enhancements

News Reading

  • Teaser in article list.
  • Support for offline images.
  • Displaying labels in article list.
  • Enable diagonal scrolling in article view on Android 2 and above.
  • Browse back button in article view.
  • Customizable action bar in article view.

Podcasts

  • Supporting lock screen playback control on Android 4 and above.
  • Highlight of last played episode in the article list.
  • Skip button on secondary playback bar.
  • Playlist always contains a playback bar for the most recently listened/next episode.
  • Option to add play list shortcut to home screen.
  • Remember approximate playback position even when killed.
  • Pause and resume using status bar.
  • Global option to automatically add new episodes to play list.
  • Option to mark finished episodes as read.
  • Migration tip for users coming from Google Listen.

Generic

  • Complete new, ICS like look and feel.
  • Introduced light, dark and black theme.
  • Menu option in article list to fetch old (read) articles from Google Reader.
  • New action bar.
  • Using native menu on Android 3 and above.
  • More comfortable share action on Android 4 and above.
  • Permanent display of scroll bars in all list views.
  • Two-pane view for settings on tablets >= Android 3.
  • Icons for main categories in settings.
  • Cloud backup of all settings on devices with market access and Android 2.2 or above.
  • User interface settings have been split up into view specific settings.
  • Storing article content in database instead of file system to prevent out of inode problem.
  • Extreme acceleration of the list view when using thousands of articles.
  • Incredible fast marking read using the tick button.
  • Overworked usage tips.
  • Several minor enhancements.
