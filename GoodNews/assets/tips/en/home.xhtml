The GoodNews start screen lists all your labels, folders and subscriptions exactly as the top level of your google reader navigation tree. Ensure that the tag/subscription sorting (alphabetic vs. drag & drop) in the view settings matches your google reader settings.

Additionally GoodNews provides a special label:

The read list provides you a place where to put articles you plan to read later on

Both lists are simple tags which will be synchronized with google reader.

Tip: In the view settings you can deactivate the special tags at the top if you don't need them.
