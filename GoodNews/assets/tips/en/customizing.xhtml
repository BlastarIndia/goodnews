]> Customizing

GoodNews is extremely customizable. So whenever something isn't solved in the way you would like to see it, you should take a look into the settings.

In each view's menu you will find two setting entries:

Settings:
These are the global settings broken down into categories like News Reading and Storage. They are the same no matter from which view you have called them.
View Settings:
These are the settings which are specific to the current view. (you will also find them in the User Interface section of the global settings)

Further on there are item specific options for labels and subscriptions available: Tap and hold such an item and select the Settings entry from it's context menu.
