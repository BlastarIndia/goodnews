]> Offline Reading and Mobilizers

Your favorite subscription only provides a teaser and not the full text? The linked website is overloaded and it is hard to find the content? Then you should enable the Readability mobilizer in the news reading settings.

Further on GoodNews supports offline reading, which enables you to read your articles (including images), even if there is no internet connection available. Read the relevant how to article to learn about all the related settings.

Tip: You can also override the global mobilizer and offline reading setting on a per subscription basis.
